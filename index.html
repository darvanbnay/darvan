About Cats

Cats are small, carnivorous mammals known for their independence, playfulness, and charm. They’ve been human companions for thousands of years!

Popular Cat Breeds

  • Siamese: Known for their striking blue eyes and vocal nature.
  • Persian: Famous for their long fur and calm demeanor.
  • Maine Coon: One of the largest domestic cat breeds, gentle giants with bushy tails.
